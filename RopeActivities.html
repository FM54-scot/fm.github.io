Introducing our Rope Based Activities

All rope-based activities take place on site with full safety equipment provided

Rope Climbing

Scale the heights of one of the local quarry slabs
Max group size: 8
Ages: 8+


Abseiling

Once you've climbed up, take the scary step and abseil from the top of one of the local quarry slabs
There is a lovely view... if you are brave enough to look down!
Max group size: 8
Ages: 8+


Pole Climbing

Ever wondered how telephone engineers get to the top of the telephone poles?
Well, here’s your chance to find out!
Max group size: 8
Ages: 8+
